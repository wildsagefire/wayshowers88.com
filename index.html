The Lightkeepers

Walking each other home.

WayShowers88 is kept by Adrian — filmmaker, tarot reader, learning astrologer — writing from the Queensland coast of Australia with Kath, his co-creator and first reader.

The whole model is on the table, because honesty is the brand: everything free here stays free. The paid readings fund a simple life — a laptop, an ocean, and the time to keep making more of this. Every reading you buy keeps the lamp lit, and we don't pretend otherwise.

No course funnels. No scarcity timers. No "manifest a Lamborghini." Just the sky, read honestly, and a light left on for whoever needs it.

Why "88"?

Eight-eight is the signature of abundance meeting itself — infinity, twice. The gate between worlds. It stands behind everything we make, quietly, the way a keel stands behind a sail.

And the lighthouse? That was never a logo. It's the job description.
